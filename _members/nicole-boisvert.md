Nicole Boisvert has been with the Pediatric Musculoskeletal Imaging Lab since 2024 and coordinates the lab’s research studies.

Nicole holds an MSc in Exercise and Health Physiology and a BSc in Exercise and Health Physiology, both from the University of Calgary. Previously, she volunteered with different research groups and studied women’s bone health.

Nicole is the lab member you’ll meet if you participate in our research! She believes that research studies are about more than just advancing science and answering questions; they are also a chance for people to engage with science and maybe learn more about themselves. She enjoys learning from and helping people understand more about their muscles and bones.

When not working, Nicole can be found crocheting, watching motorsports or Star Trek, walking her dog, and cuddling her cats.
